Frequently asked

Where is my data stored?
On your device, synced privately through your own iCloud. There is no Steerlog account and no Steerlog server.

How do I back up?
iCloud sync is automatic when you're signed into iCloud. You can also export a backup file anytime: Settings ▸ Backup & Sync ▸ Export Backup File. CSV and PDF export are free, forever.

New phone?
Install Steerlog and sign into the same iCloud — your garage comes back automatically. Or tap "Coming back? Restore your data" on the welcome screen to restore from a backup file.

Restore a purchase?
Settings ▸ Purchases ▸ Restore Purchases.
