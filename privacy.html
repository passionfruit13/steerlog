Steerlog Privacy Policy

Effective: July 18, 2026

The short version: Steerlog collects no data about you. None.

What we collect

Nothing. Steerlog has no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no ads, and no servers. We never see your vehicles, your service records, your photos, or anything else you enter.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter is stored on your device. If you're signed into iCloud, your data also syncs to your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit and iCloud key-value storage — private storage that belongs to your Apple ID. Only you and Apple's infrastructure touch it; the developer has no access to it whatsoever.

Purchases

The optional Steerlog Pro unlock is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We receive no payment details. Apple's own privacy policy governs the transaction.

Notifications

Service reminders use local notifications scheduled on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

Exported files

Backup, CSV, and PDF files you export are created on your device and go only where you choose to send them.

Children

Steerlog collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted at this address with a new effective date. Given the app's design — no data collection — meaningful changes are unlikely.
